It is a behavioral reset designed to reduce overstimulation from instant gratification.

It aims to lower the brain’s reward tolerance so a person can regain focus, lower anxiety and enjoy everyday activities again.

**Hacks which help dopamine detox:**
* Identify Time Blocks and Set Time Bounds
* Engage in low stimulus activities
* Meditate / Touch some grass.
* Physically Remove distractions

**Dopamine:**
* Critical neurotransmitter and hormone in the human body that facilitates communications between nerve cells.

**Imbalance and Health Conditions**
* Low levels - Parkinson’s, ADHD, Restless Legs Syndrome
     ↳ affects focus and motivation
 * High levels - Elevated dopamine - Schizophrenia

Addictive substances and intense behaviors force massive, unnatural surges of dopamine, “teaching” the brain to prioritize these over healthy and everyday tasks.
   ↳ might relate to why stressful situations feel like that and why the absence feels strange.
